Driveway Surface Restoration


Driveway resurfacing offers a affordable solution to restore your weathered driveway without the need for a full replacement. This process involves applying a new layer of asphalt or concrete over your existing driveway, addressing surface issues while maintaining the underlying structure. Resurfacing offers several advantages over complete reinstallation:

  • - Cost-effectiveness: Resurfacing can be considerably less expensive than a full replacement. While costs differ, resurfacing generally costs between $1 to $3 per square foot, compared to the more expensive costs of a new driveway.
  • - Quicker completion: The process is usually quicker than a full replacement, minimizing disruption to your everyday life.
  • - Prolonged lifespan: A properly executed resurfacing job can add years to your driveway's life.
  • - Improved aesthetics: Resurfacing can dramatically enhance your home's curb appeal.
  • However, if your driveway has extensive damage, big potholes, or major foundation problems, a full replacement might be more appropriate. Our specialists at Driveway Paving Experts #State can evaluate your driveway and recommend the best solution, ensuring you get the best return for your investment.

Driveway Sealing


Driveway sealing is a vital step in preserving the durability and appearance of your driveway. At Driveway Paving Experts #State, we understand the value of this protective measure and offer premium sealing services to protect your investment.

Frequency of sealing depends on the type of driveway material. For asphalt driveways, we recommend sealing once every 2-3 years. Concrete driveways, on the other hand, benefit from sealing every four to five years. However, conditions such as climate, usage, and exposure to elements may necessitate more frequent sealing.

The advantages of routine driveway sealing are many:

  • 1. Longer lifespan: Proper sealing can increase the life of your driveway by up to 20 years when used as part of a regular care program.
  • 2. Enhanced appearance: Sealing restores the deep, deep color of asphalt and gives concrete a clean, like-new look.
  • 3. Increased resistance: A sealed driveway is better prepared to withstand oil stains, water penetration, and UV damage.
  • 4. Affordable maintenance: Regular sealing can avoid costly repairs or premature replacement of your driveway.
  • At Driveway Paving Experts #State, we use only the premium sealants and employ skilled professionals to ensure your driveway receives the best protection possible. Choose the durability of your driveway with our expert sealing services.
